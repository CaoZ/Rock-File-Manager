• Enter: perform default action
• Backspace/Del: delete
• Space: move down, can be hold down
• Alt + Space/Movement: fast move
• Alt + T: move to top
• Alt + B: move to bottom

• S: search
• X: cut
• C: copy
• V: paste
• R: rename
• P: properties

* According to the type of keyboard, some shortcuts may be unavailable on some devices.
